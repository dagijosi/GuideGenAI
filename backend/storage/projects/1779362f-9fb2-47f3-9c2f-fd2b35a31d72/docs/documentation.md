# ERP

## Overview
The web application "ERP" at https://erp.loomaddis.com/ is a modern ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) and POS (Point of Sale) system tailored for businesses in Ethiopia. The homepage provides key actions such as accessing pricing details, exploring the features offered, learning about other stores using the service, logging in, or starting a new account to get up and running quickly. Additionally, it offers links to important legal documents including the Privacy Policy and Terms and Agreement pages, which allow users to review their rights and obligations regarding data privacy and usage of the platform.

## Getting Started
# Getting Started Guide for New Users of LoomERP

## Overview
Welcome to LoomERP! This modern ERP & POS system is designed to streamline your business operations in Ethiopia. Follow this guide to get started with LoomERP.

---

### Step 1: Explore Pricing and Features
1. **Navigate to the Pricing Page**:
   - Click on "Pricing" from the main menu.
2. **Understand the Options**:
   - Review the different pricing plans available for your business needs.
3. **Explore Features**:
   - Scroll through the features listed under each plan to understand what is included.

### Step 2: Choose a Store
1. **Navigate to Explore Stores Page**:
   - Click on "Explore Stores" from the main menu or directly from the Pricing page.
2. **Select Your Store**:
   - Browse through the available stores and choose one that best suits your business requirements.
3. **Note Down Store Details**:
   - Take note of any specific details about the store you have selected for future reference.

### Step 3: Log In to LoomERP
1. **Access Login Page**:
   - Click on "Login" from the main menu or directly after exploring stores.
2. **Enter Your Credentials**:
   - Enter your username and password provided by LoomERP or any administrator.
3. **Log In**:
   - Click the login button to access your account.

### Step 4: Review Privacy Policy
1. **Access Privacy Policy Page**:
   - Click on "Privacy Policy" from the main menu or directly after logging in.
2. **Read the Policy**:
   - Carefully read through the privacy policy to understand how your data will be handled.
3. **Navigate Back**:
   - Use the back button to return to the previous page.

### Step 5: Agree to Terms and Conditions
1. **Access Terms and Agreement Page**:
   - Click on "Terms and Agreement" from the main menu or directly after logging in.
2. **Read the Agreement**:
   - Read through the terms and conditions thoroughly.
3. **Agree to the Terms**:
   - Click the "I agree to these terms" button to confirm your acceptance.

### Step 6: Start Using LoomERP
1. **Explore the Interface**:
   - Familiarize yourself with the layout and features of the application.
2. **Start Your Business Operations**:
   - Begin using LoomERP for managing your ERP & POS needs.

---

By following these steps, you will be well on your way to effectively utilizing LoomERP for your business operations in Ethiopia. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team.
