# ERP

## Overview
LoomERP is described as a modern ERP and POS system available at <https://erp.loomaddis.com/>. The primary page provides an overview of the product, emphasizing its capabilities in both enterprise resource planning and point-of-sale management. Visitors can navigate to additional pages such as the Privacy Policy and Terms and Agreement, which offer important information regarding data protection and user agreements. Registration and login options are also provided, allowing users to access the full functionality of the system.

## Getting Started
### Getting Started Guide for New Users of LoomERP

#### Step 1: Accessing the Application
To begin using LoomERP, you first need to access the application. You can do this by visiting the main page titled "LoomERP - Modern ERP & POS System."

#### Step 2: Logging In
Once on the main page, look for the "Login" option. Click on it to proceed to the login screen.

1. **Enter Your Credentials**: On the login page, you will need to enter your username and password.
2. **Submit**: After entering your credentials, click the "Login" button to access the application.

#### Step 3: Understanding the Privacy Policy
After logging in, if you are unsure about how your data is being handled, you can visit the "Privacy Policy | LoomERP" page by clicking on the "Back" or "Privacy Policy" link from the main page.

1. **Read the Policy**: Take some time to read through the privacy policy to understand how your information will be managed.
2. **Return to Main Page**: Once you have reviewed the policy, click the "Back" button to return to the main page.

#### Step 4: Agreeing to Terms and Conditions
To fully utilize LoomERP, you need to agree to its terms and conditions. You can find this option on the main page by clicking on "Terms and Agreement | LoomERP."

1. **Review the Terms**: Click on "I agree to these terms" to view the agreement.
2. **Confirm Your Agreement**: After reviewing, click the "I agree to these terms" button to confirm your acceptance.

#### Step 5: Exploring the Application
Now that you have logged in and agreed to the terms, you can start exploring LoomERP's features.

1. **Main Features**: The main page provides an overview of what LoomERP offers. Take some time to familiarize yourself with its various modules and functionalities.
2. **Navigating Further**: If you need more detailed information or specific help, use the "Back" button to navigate through different sections within the application.

#### Conclusion
Congratulations! You have successfully started using LoomERP. With a basic understanding of logging in, reviewing policies, agreeing to terms, and exploring features, you are now ready to dive deeper into the full capabilities of this modern ERP & POS system.
